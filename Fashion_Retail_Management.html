Fasion Retail Management

Assignment Brief

This assignment involved creating a Risk Register presentation, for a brand of our choice, discussing day to day risks, external disruptors, and disruptive strategic risks accompanied by a Resilience Playbook outlining affective solutions to mitigate the identified risks.

Personal Process

When assigned this task, I leveraged networks gained from my recent work experience at Harrods to engage in primary research. Conducting an interview with Eve Sutherland, a Trade Assistant at Harrods, I aimed to uncover struggling brands within the industry. This interview became the focal point of my report, specifically delving into Burberry's situation by analysing their 2023 financial report.

Initially, grappling with the intricacies of financial terminology presented a challenge. However, drawing on the support of my peers, many of whom possessed prior studies in Business or Economics at A-level, facilitated a swift adaptation.

This collective knowledge allowed me to discern the risks threatening Burberry's business model, culminating in the creation of a presentation and a comprehensive Resilience Playbook.

I was proud to receive a 1:1 grade on this assignment affirming the effectiveness of my research, analysis, and presentation skills.

Skills Learned

Over the duration of this module I acquired a diverse set of valuable skills:

  • Enhanced my primary research skills
  • Proficiency in financial terminology comprehension
  • Risk identification capabilities
  • Strong presentation skills
