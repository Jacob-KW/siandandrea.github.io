Assignment Brief

We were tasked with analysing the recent impact of major events on the economic and financial performance of UK and Global Fashion Retail organisations, connecting key economic theories, concepts, and government policies directly to their trading and evolution in both UK and international markets. We also had to use a mix of primary and secondary research sources, including visual and contextual data, for a concise yet detailed report on the dynamics within the fashion retail industry during this period.
