Assignment Brief

Select and showcase a notable individual or group associated with a chosen trend. Conduct research to gather visuals and information, culminating in a 3-4 minute video presentation with voice-over and bullet points. The goal is to establish the selected personality as the definitive 'face' of the trend through a creative and informative approach.

Personal Process

As one of my first assignments, I was eager to get started. I really enjoyed this assignment as it was very creative. I immediately knew I wanted to speak about Coco Chanel and her iconic faux pearls and got started researching.

This assignment provided me with many of the base skills for all of my assignments to come such as referencing and research skills as well as providing me with video editing experience.
