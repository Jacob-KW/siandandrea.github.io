Assignment Brief

Given the opportunity to be selected to present at ASOS's headquarters, we were assigned with creating a trend report for AW/23 within a chosen accessories department, showcasing our knowledge of sourcing, manufacturing, sustainability, and cost as well as discussing and distinguishing between concept, and market led trends.

Personal Process

Upon hearing about the opportunity to present at the ASOS headquarters I immediately knew that it was an experience that I wanted to take part in. It was a great motivator and really prompted me to think outside of the project brief itself in order to ensure my project stood out.

This module introduced me to WGSN and its wealth of industry reports. I explored every lingerie trend prediction report I could find for 2023 and created a comprehensive word document with all my findings before compiling the most common and relevant ones into my report. I absolutely loved the blend of creativity in terms of presentation and the depth of research needed for forecasting.

After having presented my report to my class I was delighted to hear that I had been selected to present to the ASOS panel. This was most definitely the highlight of this module. I was proud to receive a high 2:1 and to be awarded first place by the ASOS panel!

Skills Learned

Over the duration of this module I acquired a diverse set of valuable skills:

  • Expertise in conducting Comparative Shopping reports
  • Proficiency in WGSN navigation for trend analysis
  • Strong grasp of price architecture and margins
  • Strong presentation skills
