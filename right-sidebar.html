Assignment Brief

Provided with a data set from an unnamed luxury women's clothing brand, we were tasked with producing a report demonstrating our knowledge of data analysis using Excel, revealing strategic insights on how the brand could achieve a competitive advantage backed by thorough research.

Personal Process

As my first experience with Excel, this assignment was intimidating, especially among peers who were already proficient in it. Recognising the gap, I dedicated extra time in small group tutorials lead by supportive tutors to grasp the fundamentals. Surprisingly, this journey sparked my interest in data analytics, and I found making the most out of the limited dataset to become an engaging pursuit.

Our class faced a challenge within Excel, prompting tutors to provide the required results. However, my desire for the specific chart lead me to explore Google Sheets. Despite Excel's capabilities, I discovered that for certain graphical representations, I found Google Sheets more conducive due to its wider array of creative formatting functions.

Achieving a high 1:1 on this assignment was a proud moment for me considering my starting point!

Skills Learned

Over the duration of this module I acquired a diverse set of valuable skills:

  • Proficiency in crafting dynamic charts using Pivot Tables and Formulas in Excel and Google Sheets
  • In depth understanding of the Analytics Maturity Model as a framework for analytical processes
  • Expertise in constructing impactful dashboards to convey insights effectively
  • Insightful knowledge of Machine Learning Concepts and their practical application in data analysis
